Ibadan

Ibadan as a city has suffered from a lot of misconceptions and fallacies, most people that pass through Ibadan on their way to Lagos always believe that they have been to Ibadan which is so wrong on all accounts. Those set of people are not to be blamed though as most people from this ancient city too have been to nooks and crannies so they do not have any idea how Ibadan can be a goldmine when it comes to tourism.

Place to visit

  1. Amala Joints:
    Amala is Ibadan number one export, infact, it has become a national discussion among the social media users. Recently an amala hangout was successfully organized in Ibadan, that is suffice to say eating amala is a major thing to do whenever you are in ibadan. Skye bank Amala joint is the number one most rated restaurant in Ibadan. Others like Iyadunni Restaurant, Ina-straight restaurant, Ile gate restaurant, Nta staff restaurant are notable mentions.
  2. Trans Amusement Park:
    If you are a family person, you have kindred spirits and looking for a cool place to relax, then, Trans Amusement Park is for you. It is situated around the same place as The University of Ibadan. It has green areas for aesthetics, bouncing castle, etc and other equipment you will normally find in a park.
  3. Agodi Gardens and Park:
    Agodi gardens is one of the most visited tourist attraction center in ibadan, it houses both zoological and botanical gardens. The species coupled with beautiful scenery gives an orgasmic experience that would make you want to visit again. Side attraction like football pitch, swimming pool and an in-house top class DJ will also give you a 10/10 experience.
  4. UI Zoological Garden Ibadan:
    Zoological Garden Ibadan is one of the most rated zoos in Nigeria and Sub-Saharan Africa. It does not need any sugar coating. It is situated inside the University of Ibadan. It houses a lot animals like Lion, Hyena, Gorilla, Birds, reptiles etc. It is definitely a place to be.
  5. Palms Mall
    Your Ibadan experience is not complete without visiting the amazing Palms Mall at Ring road ibadan. Apart from the fact that the biggest shoprite is located there, it has a lot of shops where one can buy souvenirs, gifts etc. there are also bouncing castles, kids playing facilities etc.
